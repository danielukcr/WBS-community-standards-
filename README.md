# Community Standards

## 1. Purpose

Our community exists to create a respectful, welcoming, and productive environment where people can share ideas, collaborate, and learn from each other. These standards outline the expectations for behaviour within this community.

By participating, you agree to follow these guidelines.

---

## 2. Respect Others

Treat all members with respect and professionalism.

### Expected Behaviour
- Be polite and constructive in discussions
- Respect differing opinions and perspectives
- Offer feedback in a helpful and supportive way
- Assume good intentions when interacting with others

### Unacceptable Behaviour
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or insults
- Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or identity
- Encouraging others to target or harass members

---

## 3. Communication Guidelines

Clear and respectful communication keeps the community productive.

### Please Do
- Stay on topic in discussions
- Use appropriate language
- Ask questions respectfully
- Provide context when requesting help

### Please Avoid
- Spam or repetitive posting
- Excessive self-promotion
- Disruptive or intentionally inflammatory comments
- Posting misleading or false information

---

## 4. Inclusivity

We aim to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome.

Members should:
- Be mindful of cultural differences
- Use inclusive language
- Respect people's identities and experiences

---

## 5. Privacy and Safety

Protect the privacy and security of others.

Do not:
- Share personal or private information without consent
- Attempt to access accounts, systems, or information you are not authorized to access
- Encourage unsafe or illegal behaviour

---

## 6. Content Standards

Content shared in the community must be appropriate and constructive.

Not permitted:
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Explicit or offensive material
- Illegal content or activities
- Content intended to provoke conflict or harm

---

## 7. Reporting Issues

If you encounter behaviour that violates these standards:

1. Report it to moderators or administrators
2. Provide relevant details or screenshots if possible
3. Do not escalate conflicts publicly

All reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially.

---

## 8. Enforcement

Moderators may take action if these standards are violated, including:

- Issuing warnings
- Removing content
- Temporary suspensions
- Permanent removal from the community

Moderation decisions are made to maintain a healthy environment for all members.

---

## 9. Updates

These standards may be updated as the community grows. Members will be notified of significant changes.

Continued participation indicates acceptance of the current standards.

---

## 10. Final Note

A strong community is built on mutual respect, cooperation, and positive participation. Every member plays a role in maintaining a welcoming environment.
